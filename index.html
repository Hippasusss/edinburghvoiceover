EDINBURGH VOICE OVER

ABOUT

Edinburgh Voice Over is a state of the art voice over recording facility located within Post Electric Studio in Leith, Edinburgh. We provide a welcoming but professional environment for you to record your voice over project.

SERVICES

Facilities

Our purpose built facilities are based in Post Electric Studio, focusing around our fully treated and isolated vocal booth, designed by Munro Acoustics. Our control room can seat multiple producers and we also provide a separate area of the building as a retreat from the main studio if required.

Services

As well as straight voice recording, we can provide: - Remote Connection (Source Connect, SessionLInkPRO, or Skype/Zoom Etc) - Voice to Picture (ADR) - Actor Voice Reels - Audio Editing - Audio Restoration If you have any other requirements then don't hesitate to get in touch.

Equipment

Second to none, we have a range of microphones including the ubiquitous Neumann U87, Neumann TLM 103, and Brauner VM1. All signals pass through high quality balanced VanDamme cable and are recorded via our SSL Preamps to either Antelope Orion32 or RME Fireface converters.
